Exercise Details

Chest & Triceps (Mon/Wed)

  • Dumbbell Chest Press (15 lb): 3 sets of 12
  • Dumbbell Flys (5 lb): 3 sets of 12
  • Overhead Tricep Extension (15 lb): 3 sets of 12
  • Push-ups: 3 sets to failure

Biceps & Shoulders (Tue/Thu)

  • Hammer Curls (15 lb): 3 sets of 12
  • Lateral Raises (5 lb): 3 sets of 12
  • Front Raises (5 lb): 3 sets of 12

Core (Tue/Thu)

  • Dumbbell Squats (15 lb): 3 sets of 12
  • Planks: 3 sets of 30-60 sec
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 (10/side)
